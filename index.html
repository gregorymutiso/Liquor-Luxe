Frequently Asked Questions

Do you deliver nationwide?

We currently deliver to all major cities in Kenya. Check your location at checkout.

What is the minimum age to order?

Customers must be 18 years or older to purchase alcoholic beverages. Age verification is required.

How long does delivery take?

Same-day delivery is available in Nairobi for orders placed before 2PM. Other locations: 1-3 business days.

Do you accept returns?

Returns are accepted on sealed, unopened items within 7 days of delivery. Contact support for processing.
